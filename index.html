A Brief Intro

This website is dedicated to Magnetic Research Group at Department of Mechatronics Engineering at Yildiz Technical Universty. The group is currently headed by Assistant Professor Kadir Erkan.

Mission

* We provide a high-quality engineering education experience that will prepare students to assume leadership positions within maglev applications of mechatronics and other associated industries.
* We encourage the pursuit of new knowledge in maglev applications.
* We carry out research in state-of- the-art laboratory facilities.
* Students are prepared for the next stage in their careers.
* Students should deeply understand the impact of their work on society.

Vision

YTU Maglev laboratory is a premier research center educating high quality engineers to serve the diverse needs of our changing society. These include the transportation and manufacturing. There are three key elements to our success.
* The laboratory has a well-funded, high quality research environment that is internationally
regarded in key areas, attracting superior faculty and students.
* The laboratory is committed to excellence in teaching and continuous improvement.
* The laboratory has strong, dynamic alliances with industry, alumni, other departments and the community.
